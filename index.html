FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

Do I own my website when it's done?
Yes — all your content, copy, and images belong to you entirely. If you ever decide to move on, we hand over everything. The only thing we retain is the underlying template license.
What if I don't have a logo or photos yet?
No problem. We can source professional stock photography that matches your brand, and we can point you to fast, affordable logo options. We work with what you have.
Can I cancel the Care Plan anytime?
After the initial 3-month minimum, you can cancel with 30 days' notice. We'll provide all your files and content — no hostage-holding, ever.
How is this different from Wix or Squarespace?
DIY builders work great if you have 20+ hours and design experience. Most business owners get frustrated and end up with something half-finished. We handle everything — and you're live in 5 days.
